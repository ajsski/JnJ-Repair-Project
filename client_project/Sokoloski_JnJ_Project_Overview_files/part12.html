Site Theme

Professional, clean and approachable.

Black text on white backgrounds. Logo supplied by client. Colors of company brand is reflex blue, gold, black and white. No other colors are to be introduced. Client has no preference on font as long as it is legible.     

Client does not own any current photos and is asking for suggestions. Once client finalizes review, we will discuss purchasing before site goes live. FPO will be placed on images until then.    

Client would like site to be mobile friendly.

The default font should be large enough to easily be parsed on any device, and scrolling should be provided on mobile, however larger screens may be able to handle the entire section content in a single screen.
