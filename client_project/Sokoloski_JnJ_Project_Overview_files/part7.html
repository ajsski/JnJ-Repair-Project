SWOT: Strengths, Weaknesses Opportunities and Threat/Challenges

Strengths:

Content (main component of the site) is already available. jandjrepair.com

John has been a part of J&J Repair for over 30 years, so he is an expert in the HVAC and small appliance field.

John has an excellent reputation on being trustworthy, knowledgable and skilled at his craft.

Weaknesses:

There is ALOT of competition out there.

Current website is dated. Copy needs updating (currently has spelling errors).

Owner does not have any web design (or web technology) knowledge.

Opportunities:

John, or his family members can create a blog page that could help keep customers informed on trends or care of equipment.

Site can have a book and appointment or estimate feature.

Threats/Challenges:

Images will need to be carefully selected as they can get dated pretty quickly if no one is updating the site often enough.
