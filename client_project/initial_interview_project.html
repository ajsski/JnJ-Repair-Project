Client Project - Analysis and Initial Interview Questions

  1. Who is the client?
  2. What need is being addressed, or what problem is being solved?
  3. What is the big goal of the project?
  4. What are the basic components that will be required to solve the problem?
  5. What technologies will be used to solve the problem?
  6. What is the time frame of the project?
  7. What are the budget constraints or what resources are needed?
  8. What are the major challenges of the project?
  9. Who are the key stakeholders/clients and members of the team?
    10.  
  10. Who is the target audience that the Key Stakeholder wants to reach?
  11. Does the Key Stakeholder want a way for the visitor of this site to reach out and ask questions directly to author by email request form?
  12. Does the Key Stakeholder have any thought on what they would like their website to look and feel like? Are there any examples of webpages they like the look or feel of another site?
  13. Does the Key Stakeholder want to be able to track traffic or statics from their site through a backstage tool or maybe an email alert? For example, an impression or how long they stayed on the page, or did they click on to go to possibly purchase the new book, etc?
